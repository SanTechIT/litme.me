About Us.

The Chamber Pots, a critically acclaimed group of string players founded by the Hart Young Talent Foundation™, are well-known around the world for their professionalism and their exquisite playing, consisting of their beautiful intonation, perfect pitch, and definitely their world-class counting skills. Following their legendary and highly successful tour in front of the world-famous Frequency 49 quintet, they performed at the Harker Middle School Chamber Concert with unparalleled gusto, creativity, and passion. Our connoisseurs noticed that Jason astonishingly was able to play his solo at the right time, a feat never accomplished before.
